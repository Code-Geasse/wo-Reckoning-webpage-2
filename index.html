About Us

We are a friendly and active community that loves to have fun and challenge ourselves. We have Bedrock Edition compatibility, so you can join us from any device. We also have daily events, updates, and giveaways to keep things fresh and exciting. Whether you want to build your base, team up with other players, or hunt down your enemies. We are a Lifesteal SMP Like server, which means we offer a semi-vanilla environment with a unique twist: every time you hit another player, you gain some of their health and they lose some of theirs. But be careful, because if you run out of hearts, you will be eliminated from the game. Can you survive in this ruthless world of PvP?

Why Choose Us

If you are looking for a challenging and exciting Minecraft experience, you should join our Lifesteal server. Lifesteal is a game mode where you can steal or lose hearts by killing or being killed by other players. This means you have to be careful and strategic in your gameplay, as you may be quickly defeated if you do not heal yourself after each fight.
